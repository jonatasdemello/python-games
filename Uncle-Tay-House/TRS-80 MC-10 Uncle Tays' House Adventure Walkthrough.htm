Well this is the end of my Retrochallenge 2014 Winter Warmup project.  I had to chase down a few last bugs in the "Uncle Tays' House Adventure" that I ported to the TRS-80 MC-10 Micro Color Basic from Javascript Applesoft Basic source that I found at github.com.  I have also now ported it to Dragon worldofdragon.org and Coco JGGAMES.zip.  I would like to thank the organizers for a very enjoyable competition and for all the impetus that it has provided.
A summary of my projects can be found at http://jimgerrie.blogspot.ca/2013/12/retrochallenge-winter-roundup.html
